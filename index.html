Trades Insurance

SD Insurance's Trades Insurance is essential for businesses involved in various trades. This insurance protects your trade business against damage, theft, and liability, ensuring that your operations can continue smoothly even in the event of an accident or other unforeseen circumstances. Whether you are a plumber, electrician, carpenter, or any other tradesperson, our Trades Insurance provides the coverage you need to keep your business running.

Key Features of Trades Insurance

  • Public Liability: Coverage for legal liability arising from injury or damage caused to third parties by your trade activities.
  • Tools and Equipment Cover: Protection against damage or theft of your tools and equipment, ensuring you can continue working without interruption.
  • Commercial Vehicle Insurance: Coverage for your work vehicles, including protection against accidents, theft, and damage.
  • Personal Accident and Illness Cover: Financial protection in case you are unable to work due to an accident or illness.
  • Property Damage: Coverage for damage to your business premises and contents caused by events such as fire, theft, and natural disasters.
  • 24/7 Claims Support: Access to a dedicated claims team available around the clock to assist you in the event of an incident.
